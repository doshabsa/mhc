What is Waves?

"Waves is a community-based stack of decentralized open-source technologies to build scalable, user-friendly apps"

Waves was created to provide an open source platform for developers to create apps that utilize blockchain. They also designed it so that it minimizes the carbon footprint that can be an issue with other blockchain technologies. Because it is open source, this project is available for anyone to take a part in developing. This is a great way for those who want to get more improved to do so, and give an opportunity to those who want to learn more about the technology.

Hacks

Waves has had their website and exchange compromised when a hacker used social engineering to acquire the login info of the companies CEO. They then used those credentials to highjack the exchange and main site to gather users personal wallet information.
